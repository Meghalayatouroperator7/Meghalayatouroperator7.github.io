Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?
Fill out the booking form on this page, or reach us via WhatsApp or phone. We respond within 24 hours with availability and confirmation details.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI (neizthangkhiew00@oksbi), bank transfer, and PayPal/card for international tourists. Full details are shared after booking confirmation.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 7 days before the tour. 50% refund for cancellations 3–7 days before. No refund for last-minute cancellations (under 3 days).
Do you provide transport?
Yes — all packages include comfortable private vehicles with experienced local drivers familiar with Meghalaya's mountain roads.
Can I customise my itinerary?
Absolutely. We specialise in tailored tours. Contact us with your dates, group size, and interests and we'll craft a personalised plan.
When is the best time to visit Meghalaya?
October–May offers pleasant weather and clear views. June–September brings spectacular waterfalls during monsoon season. Each season has its own magic!

Our Story

About Traverse Meghalaya

Traverse Meghalaya is based in Sohra (Cherrapunjee) — the place that receives the world's highest rainfall. It was founded by the seven brothers of the Dienhdoh family, who have lived and guided in this region their entire lives.

After years showing travellers the hidden corners of Meghalaya, the brothers built this company to give every visitor the deepest, most authentic experience this incredible region can offer.

  • Deliver genuinely memorable guest experiences
  • Foster cultural awareness and exchange
  • Help travellers discover Meghalaya's hidden beauty
  • Connect visitors with local communities
