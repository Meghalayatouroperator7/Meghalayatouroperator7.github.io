Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?
Fill out the booking form on this page, or contact us directly via WhatsApp or phone. We'll respond within 24 hours with availability and confirmation details.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI (neizthangkhiew00@oksbi), bank transfer, and PayPal/card for international tourists. Full payment details are shared after booking confirmation.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 7 days before your tour starts. 50% refund for cancellations 3–7 days before. No refund for last-minute (under 3 days) cancellations.
Do you provide transport?
Yes — all our tour packages include comfortable, private vehicles with experienced local drivers familiar with Meghalaya's mountain roads.
Can I customise my itinerary?
Absolutely. We specialise in tailored tours. Contact us with your dates, group size, and interests and we'll build a personalised plan for you.
What is the best time to visit Meghalaya?
October to May offers pleasant weather and clear views. June to September is monsoon season — spectacular waterfalls and lush greenery, but some routes may be challenging. Each season has its own magic!

Our Story

About Traverse Meghalaya

Traverse Meghalaya is a travel company based in Sohra (Cherrapunjee) — the place that receives the world's highest rainfall. It was founded by the seven brothers of the Dienhdoh family, who have lived here their whole lives.

After years guiding travellers across Meghalaya and beyond, the brothers built this company to give every visitor access to the deepest, most authentic experience this incredible region can offer.

  • Deliver genuinely memorable guest experiences
  • Foster cultural awareness and exchange
  • Help travellers explore Meghalaya's hidden beauty
  • Connect visitors with local communities
