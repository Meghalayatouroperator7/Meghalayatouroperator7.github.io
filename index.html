About us

Meghalaya Nomad Tours is a travel company based in Sohra (Cherrapunjee), Meghalaya. It was founded in 2022 by a youth born and brought up in these hills.

After 5-6 years as a guide exploring Meghalaya and beyond, he started this company with an aim to lend a helping hand to any traveller who wants to visit Meghalaya. Our team is comprised of local individuals with deep knowledge of the locations.
