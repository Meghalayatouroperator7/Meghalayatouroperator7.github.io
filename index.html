About us

Meghalaya Tour Operator is a travel company based in Sohra (Cherrapunjee), Meghalaya – the world's heaviest rainfall place on earth. It was founded by the seven brothers of the Dienhdoh family who reside in this region.

After years of experience as guides exploring Meghalaya and beyond, the brothers started this company with an aim to lend a helping hand to any traveller who wants to visit Meghalaya. Our team is comprised of local individuals with deep knowledge of the locations.
